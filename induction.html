Troubleshooting

Teams Audio troubleshooting:
  • Switch input/output devices in "Device Settings" and system preferences
  • Check if muted/unmuted in both application and device. Check device volume settings
  • Restart application
  • Restart computer
Onenote Syncing
  • Wait a few minutes! Syncing sometimes takes time.
  • Check if internet connected and resync by right clicking the notebook.
  • Connect using browser version
Miscellanenous problems with MySchool
  • Missing timetable:
    • Contact Mr. Kaye at the Data Office
    • The data office is located opposite Ms. Edwards' office on the first floor.
  • MySchool Assignment locked
    • Contact your teacher through Teams AND email
  • Grades/reports not showing
    • Wait until the end of the school day. It takes time for all the reports to be uploaded.
    • If still not up by the end of the day, contact Mr. Kaye/data office
Unable to join Flipgrid
  • Check if you have the correct code
  • Connect with a different device
  • Ask your teacher to send the code again
