1st February, 2018

Founding of the FRC team

The FRC team in Dulwich has been created! This is the first team for this competition ever.

2nd February, 2018

The FRC team meets for the first time

The founding members of the FRC team meet together for the first time to discuss their team name, the members required, and how to get funding

3rd February 2018

Website creation

The FRC programming team creates the official FRC website. It isn't perfect, but will build up with time to make a work of art

4th February, 2018

A team name is found

The FRC has, after long hours of discussion, agreed upon their official team name that will go before them wherever they go: Helion Robotics!

5th February, 2018

The competition begins...

After weeks and weeks of hard work and labour, the Helion Robot is ready. Now, chosen members of the team are taking it to Silicon ValleyGood Luck!
